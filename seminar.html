About the Seminar

Join Olympic Athlete and French Champion Dylan Chellamootoo for an intensive sparring seminar focused on advanced techniques and strategies. This exclusive session is designed for serious practitioners looking to elevate their game.

What You'll Learn

  • Advanced sparring techniques and combinations
  • Strategic positioning and movement
  • Defensive tactics and counter-attacks
  • Mental preparation and focus
  • Competition-specific training methods

Requirements

  • Minimum 2 years of training experience
  • Own equipment (gloves, mouthguard, etc.)
  • Signed waiver form
  • Current medical clearance

Meet Your Instructor

Dylan Chellamootoo

Olympic Athlete • French Champion • European Medalist

With over 15 years of competitive experience, Dylan has trained and competed at the highest levels of martial arts. His teaching methodology combines traditional techniques with modern sports science to help athletes reach their full potential.
