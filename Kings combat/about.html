About Kings Combat

Elevating Combat Sports to New Heights

Our Mission

Kings Combat is dedicated to promoting excellence in combat sports through high-quality events, professional training, and community building. We strive to create an environment where athletes can showcase their skills, learn from the best, and grow in their martial arts journey.

Event Overview

The Kings Combat Event 2025 is a premier Taekwondo tournament bringing together athletes from various disciplines to compete and share their passion for martial arts.

Meet Your Host

Dylan Chellamootoo

Olympic Athlete • French Champion • European Medalist

  • French National Champion
  • European Championship Medalist
